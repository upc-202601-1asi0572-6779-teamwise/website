Key Benefits

Everything your farm needs, built for the Amazon

Four pillars that make SmartPalm the most reliable agronomic intelligence platform for tropical palm oil cultivation.

100% Offline Operation

Edge computing + LoRaWAN mesh means your nodes collect and process data locally. No mobile signal, no Wi-Fi, no problem — intelligence lives at the farm, not in the cloud.

Critical Real-Time Alerts

Three-tier alert system (Informational → Warning → Critical) for water stress, phytosanitary events, and extreme weather. Get notified under 2 seconds via mobile notification.

AI + INIA Parameters

Our agronomic engine is calibrated with INIA's research data for Amazonian palm oil varieties — delivering recommendations aligned with Peru's best agricultural science.

Exportable Technical Reports

Generate PDF and CSV agronomic reports per block, per period, or per node — ready to share with cooperatives, certifying bodies, or financial institutions.
